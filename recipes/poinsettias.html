Poinsettias

Description

This is a great holiday cocktail to get the Christmas season started!; fresh and springy cranberry juice mixed with your choice of brut champagne makes for a whimsical and charming holiday experience.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces brut champagne
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons cranberry juice

Steps:

  1. Pour champagne into a champagne flute, and top with cranberry juice.
  2. Garnish with sugar coated cranberries, or as desired.

That's it!! Congratulations, you have made a festive cocktail fit for the cheerful Christmas holiday!!
